About me
I am a fifth year Ph.D student supervised by Simon Lacoste-Julien. I have graduated from Université de Montréal with a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Economics. My current focus is on questions of identifiability in representation learning. I also did work on causal structure learning with continuous-constrained approaches. More details can be found in my resume.
